About Me

Hi, I’m Alyaa, a Diploma in Information Management student currently in my fifth semester. I have four siblings and I am the fourth child. I live in Kampung Padang Janggus, a peaceful village near a beautiful waterfall. I love cats and I consider myself an introvert — but once I feel comfortable, I can be the complete opposite!

I enjoy spending my alone time watching my favourite series, The Rookie. I love English songs, and sometimes a few Malay songs too. My favourite subject has always been English because it feels natural and exciting to learn.

When it comes to assignments, I strangely work best under pressure. My brain becomes more creative when deadlines are close — not the best habit, but it works for me!

Lastly, I want to express my gratitude to everyone who has supported me throughout this roller coaster academic journey, especially ALLAH for His guidance and blessings.
